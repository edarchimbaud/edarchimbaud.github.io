Why it works
This strategy leverages the well-documented momentum effect to achieve appealing return/risk characteristics. It employs a minimum variance optimization technique, which is enhanced by using short-term data for more stable correlations and volatilities. The approach utilizes weekly rebalancing and selects the top-performing assets based on their 6-month momentum. The portfolio is then optimized for minimal variance using 60-day historical data and adjusted to target an 8% volatility. This method offers a reliable and effective way to navigate the investment universe.
Hedging stocks in bear markets?
The chosen strategy belongs to the ‚ÄúTactical Asset Allocation‚Äù (TAA) category, similar to the approach proposed by Mebane Faber. It includes equities and aims to rotate out of them during periods of market stress. This strategy is not primarily used as a direct hedge for equity risk, but rather as an overlay for managing the percentage of equities in a portfolio. The TAA framework can reduce the overall risk associated with equities and enhance risk-adjusted returns in a portfolio.
