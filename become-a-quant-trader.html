Become a quant trader
I started running my own automated trading programs from my dorm room a few years ago. I am starting this website about automated trading to document ideas and hopefully meet some interesting people.
The purpose of this website is to give you the knowledge to get a job in the field. That is a vast body of information but I will provide you with enough to be a great asset to any quant group. I will advise you on which companies to join, how the interviews work, and what other skills you should develop before applying.
I expect the audience for this script will be pretty diverse. It has parts that are inappropriate to some readers, but I know most quants are already used to jumping around when they read books about trading to try to extract all the useful bits.
The best way to read this script is to jump to sections you are interested in. If you are completely new to quant trading, read each section in order.
